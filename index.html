Facebook

Facebook is a social media platform owned by Meta platforms with Mark Zuckerberg as its CEO. Just until recently the whole company was named Facebook but recently changed to meta. It is one of the biggest social media platforms today. Facebook allows people to share photos and videos, connect with friends and loved ones, and join interest groups. In addition, Facebook also provides other services such as marketplace where users can buy or sell items in the local community. According to Wikipedia, “Facebook is an online social media and social networking service owned by American company Meta Platforms. Founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg with fellow Harvard College students and roommates Eduardo Saverin, Andrew McCollum, Dustin Moskovitz, and Chris Hughes, its name comes from the Facebook directories often given to American university students.” Facebook was originally developed for use in universities but now anyone can access it. According to Statista.com “In 2022, the revenue general by Meta Platforms (formerly known as Facebook Inc.) amounted to roughly 116.6 billion US dollars. It is estimated that Facebook had over 2.96 billion active users in the third quarter of 2022.” bernardmarr.com posed some more interesting facts about Facebook like, "there are five new Facebook profiles created every second, More than 300 million photos get uploaded per day and every minute there are 510,000 comments posted and 293,000 statuses updated."" Despite its popularity Facebook has had its fare share of controversies and has received a lot of criticism especially regarding its privacy, data collection and misinformation and fake news. Nonetheless Facebook has become one of the most influential tech company in the world and helped many people connect to their loved ones
